ProCon

Jennifer Wade

As the owner and operator Jennifer Wade brings over twenty years experience in finance, human resource management and workforce development. Jennifer consults with small to medium size organizations with a passion for assisting emerging businesses in developing practices, processes and procedures. After serving in the united states Army she attained the Master of Science degree in Human Resource and Organization Development from University of Louisville. In May 2018, her dream was fulfilled when she launched ProCon LLC, consulting.
